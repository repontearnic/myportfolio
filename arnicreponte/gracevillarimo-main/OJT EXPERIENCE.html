OJT EXPERIENCE

The purpose of selecting this specific internship opportunity was to acquire practical experience and immerse myself in the IT industry. My intention was to put into practice the knowledge and skills I acquired within the confines of the classroom, thereby bridging the gap between theory and real-world scenarios. Moreover, my objective was to leverage the internship as a platform for learning from seasoned professionals and gaining valuable insights into the current trends and advancements shaping the industry. The internship was a deliberate choice to gain firsthand exposure to the challenges and dynamics of the IT field. By actively participating in real-world situations, I aimed to enhance my problem-solving abilities and adapt my theoretical knowledge to practical contexts. Additionally, working alongside experienced professionals during the internship provided me with an invaluable opportunity to observe their expertise and acquire valuable lessons from their vast industry experience. This exposure also allowed me to stay informed about the latest trends, emerging technologies, and innovative practices, keeping me at the forefront of industry developments. Overall, the internship was a strategic decision to foster professional growth and broaden my understanding of the IT industry beyond the confines of the classroom.

Assuming the role of the lead profiler brought forth a formidable challenge, as we were tasked with completing a demanding quota of 1,200 tasks within a tight eight-hour deadline. Despite the potential monotony that could arise from such work, our enduring fondness for our responsibilities stemmed from the unwavering support and collaboration amongst our colleagues. In moments when the workload seemed overwhelming, we found solace in the camaraderie of our team, as they graciously extended their helping hands whenever necessary. Their willingness to assist not only lightened the burden but also fostered a sense of unity and shared purpose, making the work environment more enjoyable and motivating. While the nature of our tasks could occasionally lead to a sense of tedium, our affinity for the work persevered due to the positive dynamics within our team. The collective effort and mutual support demonstrated by our coworkers created a conducive atmosphere that fueled our passion for the job. The synergy we experienced, coupled with the shared determination to meet the demanding quotas, allowed us to overcome challenges together and accomplish our goals effectively. Ultimately, the invaluable support from our colleagues acted as a driving force, transforming what could have been a mundane experience into a rewarding journey of personal growth and professional achievement.

During my internship, I had the opportunity to be part of a prominent BPO firm that specialized in handling outbound and incoming calls for businesses across various scales. The company boasted a dedicated team of proficient call center agents, a knowledgeable team leader, HR officers, and other supporting staff members, all committed to delivering top-notch solutions to customers. Their expertise and professionalism ensured that clients received exceptional service and support throughout their interactions with the organization. Within this BPO firm, the call center agents showcased remarkable skills in effectively communicating with customers, addressing their inquiries, and providing tailored solutions. Their proficiency in managing both outbound and incoming calls played a vital role in maintaining positive customer relationships. Supported by a team leader who provided guidance and oversight, they worked collaboratively to ensure efficient call handling and deliver high-quality service that met the diverse needs of businesses. Additionally, the BPO firm recognized the significance of a well-rounded support structure and employed HR officers and other staff members who contributed to the smooth functioning of the organization. These professionals played crucial roles in various areas, such as recruitment, training, employee management, and administrative tasks. Their collective efforts were instrumental in creating an environment conducive to customer satisfaction and operational excellence. Together, the skilled call center agents, team leader, HR officers, and supporting staff formed a cohesive unit that upheld the company's reputation for delivering exceptional customer service and solutions.

On-the-job training, commonly referred to as an internship or work placement, is essential for preparing students for the demands and challenges of the IT sector in the real world. It bridges the gap between academic knowledge and practical application while providing me with useful skills, industry exposure, and networking possibilities.
