The field of information technology requires strong reasoning skills to analyze complex problems and develop logical solutions. During my coursework, I have encountered numerous programming challenges and logical puzzles that have honed my ability to think critically and reason effectively. Problem Solving

Information technology is innately focused on fixing problems. I've had to come up with efficient answers to a variety of real-world situations as I've gone through my coursework.
